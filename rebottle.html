ReBottle App UX/UI Design

The app is designed to encourage Madrid citizens to recycle plastic bottles and build long-term sustainability habits by gamifying the experience. User’s act of recycling will be justified by earning coins and redeeming into physical rewards.

Timeline: Apr 26 - May 16 (3 weeks)

Tools: LEGO│Pencils & Post-its│Miro│Figma

Challenge

In Madrid, people don’t have awareness regarding recycling, they mix all trash together, neglecting recycling. This leads to waste of recyclable materials, harming the environment and waste management efforts.

The Vision

  1. Build a sustainable habit of plastic bottle recycling among users, driving long-term behavioral change.
  2. Incentivize users to bring their plastic bottles to our recycling points.
  3. Measure Success by the amount of plastic bottles recycled monthly.

Reflection

This was my first experience tackling an issue using gamification mechanics. Starting the brainstorming process with LEGOs allowed my team and me to approach the problem from a physical service perspective before considering the digital solution. This approach encouraged us to think more holistically, as I believe digital solutions should complement physical services to truly impact people’s lives. While my focus is on UX/UI design, I strongly advocate for beginning with the physical service and then determining how digital solutions can enhance its functionality and ease of use.
